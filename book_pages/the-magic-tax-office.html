The Magic Tax Office

Death, taxes, and unregistered necromancy.


Arthur Pendelton thought auditing magical entities would be a quiet desk job. Instead, he spends his days dealing with tax-evading dragons, wizards trying to write off doomsday devices as business expenses, and the occasional cursed ledger. But when a massive discrepancy in the kingdom's magical reserves points to a sinister plot, Arthur and his goblin intern must balance the books before reality itself goes bankrupt.

Read if you love cozy urban fantasy, bureaucratic humor, and the thrilling tension of a perfectly executed tax audit against a dark lord.
