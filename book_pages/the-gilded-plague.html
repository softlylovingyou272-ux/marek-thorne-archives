AN ALCHEMICAL HORROR NOVEL

The Gilded Plague

A beautiful death is still a death.


In the opulent heights of the capital, the aristocracy is succumbing to a terrifying new affliction: their veins harden, their eyes shimmer, and their blood literally turns to liquid gold. Disgraced alchemist Silas Vane is tasked with finding a cure in the city's underbelly, but he soon realizes the plague isn't a disease—it's a targeted reckoning. As the city descends into glittering madness, Silas must decide whether to save the elite who ruined him, or let the golden rot take them all.

Read if you crave alchemical horror, Victorian opulence, and a deadly mystery where greed is literally a disease.
