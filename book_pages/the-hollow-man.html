The Hollow Man

He is the gap between seconds. The shadow you forget when you blink.


Detective Elias Vance is hunting a serial killer who leaves no evidence, no witnesses, and no trace of identity. But as the investigation deepens, Vance's own life begins to unravel. Photographs of him fade. His records vanish from the precinct. When his own partner fails to recognize him, Vance realizes the horrifying truth: he isn't just hunting the Hollow Man—he is being systematically erased by him.

Read if you crave existential dread, supernatural noir, and the terrifying concept of being completely forgotten while you are still alive.
