A NAUTICAL HORROR NOVEL

The Ghost-Ship Privateers

Dead men tell no tales, but their ships still carry gold.


Captain Malachi Flint and his crew don't sail the ocean—they sail the Ethereal Tides. Aboard the *Wraith*, a privateer vessel forged from spectral timber, they hunt rogue phantom ships for the Crown. But when they intercept a legendary galleon carrying a cursed artifact that can bridge the world of the living and the dead, Flint realizes some treasures are better left at the bottom of the abyss. Now, with a mutinous crew and the royal armada on his tail, he must sail into the heart of the afterlife to survive.

Read if you crave swashbuckling nautical horror, spectral naval battles, and a crew of misfits who rob the dead to pay their bar tabs.
